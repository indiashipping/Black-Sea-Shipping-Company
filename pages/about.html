About Us

Black Sea Shipping Company is a privately owned international freight forwarder and logistics provider. During its thirty-plus year history, we have experienced exponential growth based on the vision and energy of its founder Mr. A.M. RAJA, who today remains Chairman, and we have built a global network of quality offices that deliver a consistent level of high service and performance to its customers.

The company was founded in 1997 in Chennai, South India. From modest beginnings by the late 1990's Black Sea Shipping Company had expanded its agents in Europe, United States and we are extending to most of the gulf countries to meet the demands of its growing customer base.

We are one of the most reckoned names offering forwarding, customs clearance, air and sea cargo shipping agent, warehousing and logistics services. We are a well known name in these services and this could be possible only by the high quality of services offered by us.

We work in close coordination with a team of talented professionals, aiming to provide diversified services which are specially designed to give our esteemed clients a competitive edge in the present market scenario. Our proficient and well-coordinated management system assures that all our consignments are delivered to the desired destination by maintaining originality in the products.

Our services are economical, which is a prime factor that has fetched victorious results and profitable ventures for our valued clients. Supporting services covering air, sea and land, we also prioritize promptness and punctuality in acquiring the highest level of satisfaction from clients across the globe.
